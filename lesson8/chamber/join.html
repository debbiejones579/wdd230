Membership Information

Any registered 501 c3 Non-Profit organization interested in the objectives of the Hooper Chamber of Commerce may become an active member upon payment of dues and shall continue as long as annual dues are paid. There shall be one designated Active Primary Representative with one vote per each Non-Profit Membership.

Any individual, sole proprietorship, independent contractor, or one-person operation with a registered business interested in the objectives of the Hooper Chamber of Commerce may become an active member upon payment of dues and shall continue as long as annual dues are paid. There shall be one designated Active Primary Representative with one vote per each Individual Membership.

Any individual, sole proprietorship, independent contractor, or one-person operation with a registered business interested in the objectives of the Hooper Chamber of Commerce may become an active member upon payment of dues and shall continue as long as annual dues are paid. There shall be one designated Active Primary Representative with one vote per each Individual Membership. Offers additional Representative, does not get additional vote.

Any registered incorporated business interested in the objectives of the Hooper Chamber of Commerce may become an active member upon payment of dues and shall continue as long as annual dues are paid. There shall be one designated Active Primary Representative with one vote per each Corporate Membership.
